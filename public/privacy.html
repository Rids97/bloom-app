Privacy Policy
We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data Bloom collects, how it is used, and how we protect it.
Last updated: April 11, 2026
01
About Bloom

Bloom ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered women's health companion developed by a licensed gynecologist, available at bloomhealth.fit and on the Google Play Store.

Bloom provides evidence-based health information, personalised guidance, and report analysis for women across fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum journeys.

⚠️ Bloom is an informational platform and does not provide medical diagnosis or replace consultation with a qualified healthcare provider.
02
Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Bloom:

Account Information:

  • Email address (used for login and account verification)
  • Password (stored as an encrypted hash — we never store plain text passwords)

Health Profile Information (provided voluntarily by you):

  • Name, age, menstrual cycle details
  • Fertility journey stage and symptoms
  • Medications and treatment history
  • Hormone test results and investigation values
  • Pregnancy and postpartum details
  • Notes and concerns you choose to share

Usage Information:

  • Chat messages sent to Bloom AI
  • Report images uploaded for analysis
  • Message count and subscription plan

Payment Information:

  • Subscription payments are processed by Razorpay. Bloom does not store your card details. Please refer to Razorpay's Privacy Policy for payment data handling.
03
How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalised AI health guidance tailored to your profile
  • Analyse medical reports you upload
  • Generate your fertility or pregnancy roadmap
  • Send OTP verification and password reset emails
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve the accuracy and safety of Bloom's responses

We do not use your health information for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties.

04
AI Processing & Third-Party Services

Bloom uses the following third-party services to deliver its features:

  • Groq API — AI language model that processes your health queries and report images. Messages are sent to Groq's servers for processing. Groq's data retention policies apply.
  • MongoDB Atlas — Cloud database where your profile and account data is stored securely.
  • Razorpay — Payment processing for Bloom Pro and Bloom Complete subscriptions.
  • Gmail (via Nodemailer) — Used to send OTP verification and password reset emails.
  • Render — Cloud hosting provider where Bloom's server runs.

All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

05
Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas (cloud servers). We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — never stored in plain text
  • All API communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) with expiry
  • Payment data is never stored on our servers

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Bloom account.

06
Health Data & Sensitive Information

Bloom handles sensitive health information including menstrual, fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum data. This information is:

  • Used solely to personalise your Bloom experience
  • Never shared with advertisers or third-party data brokers
  • Stored securely in our database
  • Accessible only to you through your authenticated account
Medical report images you upload are sent to our AI model for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond the processing session.
07
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View the health profile data stored about you
  • Update — Edit or correct your profile information at any time
  • Delete — Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent — Stop using Bloom at any time

To request account deletion or a copy of your data, email us at support@bloomhealth.fit.

08
Children's Privacy

Bloom is intended for use by adults (18 years and older). We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

09
Cookies & Local Storage

Bloom uses browser local storage (not cookies) to store your login token and app preferences locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to third parties.

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Bloom after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
